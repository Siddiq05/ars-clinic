    Dental Laminates

    If you’ve ever wondered why so many individuals in public life have such perfect smiles, you might be surprised to find out how many of them have had cosmetic dental work!

    One way that we can make your smile more attractive is to place porcelain laminate veneers or jacket crowns. Porcelain veneers are translucent, thin laminates made to improve the aesthetics (shape, spacing, color) of natural teeth, to correct crowding, or to replace composite bonding.

    If the alignment and bite are not suitable for laminates, metal-free jacket crowns are often a more reliable, long-term solution that requires less maintenance. It's important to consult with your dentist to determine the best option for you.

    Procedure

    During the first consultation, your x-rays and visual inspection, along with the available time for treatment, will determine the best course of action—whether it's porcelain, lumineers, veneers, jacket crowns, or pre-cosmetic orthodontic correction followed by laminates.

    Please visit the Crown and Bridges section for more details on the process.

    Time Required

    The time required for the procedure depends on the overall treatment plan, including whether orthodontics is involved. The laminates procedure itself typically takes about a week.

    Treatment Charges

    Porcelain laminates and crowns typically cost between Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 12,000 per tooth.

    FAQ's

    Does Laminate Make Your Teeth Weaker?

    Dental laminates do not weaken your teeth; in fact, they strengthen them by protecting against decay and damage. Fragile teeth can be prone to chipping, but laminates help reinforce them through bonding.

    Are Dental Laminates Permanent or Temporary?

    Dental laminates are a permanent solution and can last between 7 to 20 years. Maintaining good oral hygiene is essential for their durability and longevity.

    Is Maintenance of Dental Laminates Easy?

    Yes! Caring for dental laminates is no different from your regular oral hygiene routine. Continue brushing, flossing, and attending regular check-ups at Chisel Dental Clinic—no special maintenance is required.

    Do Dental Laminates Stain?

    No! Dental laminates have polished surfaces that make them resistant to staining. You can enjoy any kind of food without worrying about discoloration.

    Is Getting Dental Laminates a Painful Procedure?

    The procedure is completely pain-free once the numbness sets in. After administering local anesthesia, you will not feel any discomfort throughout the treatment.

    When Are Dental Laminates Not Recommended?

    If the alignment and bite are not suitable for laminates, metal-free jacket crowns may be a better long-term, maintenance-free option. Your dentist can help determine the best choice for you.

    What Changes Can I Expect from a Dental Laminate Procedure?

    Dental laminates are widely used to enhance the shape and color of teeth. For excessive positional changes, jacket crowns or pre-cosmetic orthodontics may be necessary.
